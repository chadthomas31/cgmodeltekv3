Homepage (/)

- Issues: Oversized logo, too many overlays, dropdown panels not keyboard accessible

Services (/services)
- Strengths: Featured service, all 10 services accessible, FAQ
- Issues: Grid layout inefficient, process not visual, FAQ collapsed
- Priority: High (conversion driver)

Equipment (/equipment)
- Strengths: All 20+ machines visible, specs shown, good summary
- Issues: No search/filter, specs not expandable, no use case tags
- Priority: Medium

About (/about)
- Strengths: Leadership team, clear mission, FAQ
- Issues: Only 4/8 have photos, bios too long, no timeline
- Priority: Low

Contact (/contact)
- Strengths: Phone visible, location with map, CTA
- Issues: Very minimal, no email option, no hours, no social links
- Priority: High (accessibility issue)

RFQ (/rfq)
- Strengths: Comprehensive form fields, success message, quick exits
- Issues: Header hidden, no breadcrumbs, no progress, file upload disabled
- Priority: Critical (highest impact on conversions)
